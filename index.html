About Alexander Robles

Hello, My name is Alex Robles. For the past 5 years I have been a Research Assistant at Beth Israel in an clinical research lab focusing on creating an effective HIV vaccine. Although I have learned that clinical research in a biological lab is not for me, during that time I have developed and improved upon many of the skills that will help me become a successful web developer. These skills include working effectively in a team oriented atmosphere, attention to detail, and a strong desire to be constantly learning and improving myself. In the short term I am hoping to find employment for a company that allows me to continue learning and to develop my skills as a developer. In the longer term I am hoping to find a position as a software developer working for either a company specializing in renewable energy or something involving the entertainment industry.

Projects

Tic-Tac-Toe

A Tic-Tac-Toe project that utilized JavaScript, jQuery, HTML, Bootstrap, and CSS to create a basic interactive Tic-Tag-Toe game. I first started with the trying to make the engine function in Node.js. Next, I turned to making the game function on the web browser, connecting each move a player performs with an action on the API. This was the most challenging aspect of the project and was accomplished over several days of slow grinding but after several iterations of AJAX code I was able to make everything work.
